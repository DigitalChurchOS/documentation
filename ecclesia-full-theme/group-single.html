Meeting weekly in the North Zone area for parenting guidance, couples bible study, corporate prayer, and supportive fellowship.

About this Group

The Northside Young Couples Fellowship is a cell group dedicated to establishing strong, Christ-centered marriages and families. We meet every Tuesday night to share dinner, discuss Scripture, pray for one another, and build authentic connections.

Whether you are newly married, preparing for marriage, or navigating the parenting of toddlers and teens, you will find a warm, supportive circle of couples here to walk alongside you. Children are welcome; we arrange childcare volunteers during the study.

Current Discussion & Study

Study: "The Power of a Spirit-Led Life"

Following Pastor Daniel Grace’s sermon series on living with confident faith, bold prayer, and obedience.
