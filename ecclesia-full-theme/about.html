About our church

A family of faith, love, worship, and purpose.

Grace City Church exists to help people know Jesus, grow in the Word, build strong families, and serve their city with compassion and excellence.

Our story

From a small prayer gathering to a growing church family.

Our church began with a simple desire: to create a place where people could encounter God’s love, receive the Word, and find a spiritual home. Today, that same vision continues through worship services, ministries, small groups, outreach, prayer, and discipleship.
