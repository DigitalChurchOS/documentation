Faith That Speaks

A sermon audio episode on prayer, confession, and standing firmly on the Word of God.

Show Notes

Episode summary

In this episode, Pastor Daniel teaches how faith must be spoken, practiced, and anchored in the Word of God. This episode includes scripture references, practical reflections, and prayer points for personal growth.

Transcript

Episode transcript

Faith is not silent. Faith agrees with God. When your heart receives the Word, your mouth gives expression to what you believe...

The pressure around you does not have the final voice. The Word of God has the final voice. This is why confession is not empty repetition; it is agreement with truth.

As you listen, ask yourself: what has God said, and what am I saying in response?
